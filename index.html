Here are types of images:

.rounded - rounds image corners

.rounded-circle - completely rounds down all corners, shapes the image to a circle

.img-thumbnail - shapes the image to a thumbnail, which adds a rounded border around it.

.float-start and .float-end

Should only be used to wrap text around images! Floats the images to the left and to the right respectively

.d-block + .mx-auto - centers the image by setting its display to block and giving it margin: auto

.img-fluid - creates a responsive image by giving it max-width: 100% and height: auto
